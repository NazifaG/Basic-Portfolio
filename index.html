About Me

Andrew was born on October 30, 1986, in New York and raised in Ridgefield, Connecticut. He began coding and designing websites at age 11. After a year of making him own websites and blogs, he made a website for kids to learn how to code using HTML and CSS and to make their own websites. Andrew became interested in coding from building a website homepage for him Neopet. Eventually his website had about 50,000 people visiting per day.

Hi, my name is Andrew Reifman. I'am freelance graphic designer and art director in Atlanta. I specialize in web and app design, front and development, user experience, and creating identities and branding. I've worked with companies of all shapes and sizes, so contact me if you'd like to work together on your next project.
